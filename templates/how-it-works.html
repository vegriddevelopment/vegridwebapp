The VeGrid Journey

A detailed look at your solar transformation

Request

Customer submits energy needs via web or mobile app. Our digital platform makes it easy to get started with just a few clicks.

Feasibility

VeGriddy conducts digital site survey and system sizing using advanced mapping and analytics tools to determine the optimal solar solution for your property.

Quotation

Customer receives transparent pricing and financing options. Our quote includes detailed information about system size, costs, savings, and payment plans.

Approval

Financier underwrites loan using VeGriddy credit scoring. Our digital credit assessment process is fast, secure, and transparent.

Procurement

Distributor fulfills order with certified equipment. We ensure all components meet industry standards and are delivered on time.

Installation

Licensed installer deploys system with remote QA. Our professional installation team ensures your solar system is set up correctly and efficiently.

Commissioning

VeGriddy validates performance and activates monitoring. We conduct comprehensive testing to ensure your system is operating at optimal efficiency.

Operations

Real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, automated billing. Our digital platform provides ongoing support and insights to maximize your solar investment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our process

The installation process typically takes 2-4 weeks from approval. This includes site assessment, design, permitting, and installation. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your business operations.

We offer a power-as-a-service model with zero upfront costs. You pay a fixed monthly fee for the solar power you use. The cost is typically 30-50% less than traditional grid electricity.

We handle all maintenance and monitoring for your solar system. Our 24/7 monitoring system detects any issues immediately, and our maintenance team responds promptly. You don't need to worry about anything.

We offer flexible terms that allow you to transfer your solar contract to a new location. Our team will assess the new property and make any necessary adjustments to the system.
