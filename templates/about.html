About VeGrid

We're on a mission to provide reliable, affordable solar power to businesses across Africa. An asset-light, software-driven platform orchestrating financing, deployment, monitoring, and payments for distributed solar at scale.

Get Your Quote Learn More

Who We Are

We own a digital technology platform — VESPMAN — that connects investors, financiers, licensed installers, distributors, and customers under a Solar Power-as-a-Service model.

We provide the digital operating system that makes every installation faster, cheaper, and more transparent.

VeGrid's core asset is its software, data, and analytics. VeGrid is a leading solar power-as-a-service provider dedicated to bringing clean, reliable energy to businesses across Africa. Our innovative approach eliminates the financial barriers to solar adoption through affordable subscription models.

Our Story

VeGrid was born out of a simple idea: energy should be reliable, affordable, and sustainable. Our founders recognized the huge potential of solar energy in Africa and the challenges businesses face with traditional power sources.

We set out to create a model that makes solar power accessible to everyone, regardless of financial constraints. Today, our solar power-as-a-service model has revolutionized how businesses access clean energy.

  • Innovative subscription model
  • Zero upfront costs
  • Fixed monthly payments
