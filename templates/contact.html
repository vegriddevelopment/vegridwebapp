Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our solar solutions

We offer a power-as-a-service model with zero upfront costs. You pay a fixed monthly fee based on your energy consumption. The cost is typically 30-50% lower than traditional grid electricity.

Our installation process typically takes 2-4 weeks from approval. We handle all permits and logistics to ensure a smooth and efficient installation.

We include comprehensive maintenance in our service. Our team handles all maintenance and monitoring, including cleaning, inspections, and repairs.

We offer flexible terms that allow you to transfer your solar system to a new location if you move your business.
